Student Visa (Tier 4)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS)
  • Sufficient financial resources (£1,334/month)
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Valid passport
  • Healthcare surcharge payment

Application Process

  • Receive CAS from institution
  • Pay healthcare surcharge
  • Complete online application
  • Submit biometrics
  • Attend visa interview if required

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • CAS statement
  • Financial statements
  • English test results
  • Academic transcripts
  • Healthcare surcharge receipt

Work Visa Options

Skilled Worker Visa

  • For sponsored skilled workers
  • Minimum salary £25,600
  • English language requirement
  • Path to settlement
  • Family can accompany

Graduate Visa

  • For recent UK graduates
  • Valid for 2-3 years
  • Work in any sector
  • No sponsorship needed
  • Path to work visa

Global Talent Visa

  • For leaders in their field
  • No job offer required
  • Fast-track to settlement
  • Flexible work options
  • Family can accompany
