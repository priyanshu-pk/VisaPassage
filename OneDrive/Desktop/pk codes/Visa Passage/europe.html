Student Visa (Schengen)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Acceptance at EU institution
  • Sufficient financial resources (varies by country)
  • Language proficiency (varies by country)
  • Valid passport
  • Health insurance coverage

Application Process

  • Receive acceptance letter
  • Pay tuition fees
  • Complete visa application
  • Submit biometrics
  • Attend visa interview

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Acceptance letter
  • Financial statements
  • Language certificates
  • Academic transcripts
  • Health insurance proof

Work Visa Options

Blue Card (EU)

  • For highly qualified workers
  • Valid in all EU countries
  • Minimum salary requirement
  • Path to permanent residency
  • Family can accompany

National Work Permits

  • Country-specific requirements
  • Employer sponsorship
  • Valid for 1-3 years
  • Renewable
  • Path to residency

Post-Study Work Rights

  • Varies by country
  • For recent graduates
  • Work in any sector
  • No sponsorship needed
  • Path to work visa
