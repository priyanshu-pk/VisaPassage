Your gateway to world-class education and career opportunities in the Land Down Under

Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Confirmation of Enrollment (CoE)
  • Sufficient financial resources (AUD$21,041/year)
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Valid passport
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)

Application Process

  • Receive CoE from institution
  • Purchase OSHC
  • Complete online application
  • Submit biometrics
  • Attend visa interview if required

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • CoE statement
  • Financial statements
  • English test results
  • Academic transcripts
  • OSHC certificate

Work Visa Options

Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)

  • Points-based system
  • No sponsorship required
  • Permanent residency
  • Work in any occupation
  • Family can accompany

Temporary Skill Shortage (Subclass 482)

  • Employer sponsorship
  • Valid for up to 4 years
  • Path to permanent residency
  • Work in nominated occupation
  • Family can accompany

Post-Study Work Visa (Subclass 485)

  • For recent graduates
  • Valid for 2-4 years
  • Work in any sector
  • No sponsorship needed
  • Path to permanent residency
