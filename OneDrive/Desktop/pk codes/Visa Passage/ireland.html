Study & Work in Ireland

Your gateway to world-class education and career opportunities in the Emerald Isle

Student Visa (Stamp 2)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Acceptance at a recognized Irish institution
  • Sufficient financial resources (€7,000 minimum)
  • Private medical insurance
  • English language proficiency
  • Valid passport

Application Process

  • Receive acceptance letter
  • Pay tuition fees
  • Complete online application
  • Submit biometrics
  • Attend visa interview if required

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Acceptance letter
  • Financial statements
  • Medical insurance
  • Academic transcripts
  • English test results

Work Visa Options

Critical Skills Employment Permit

  • For highly skilled workers
  • Minimum salary €32,000
  • Path to permanent residency
  • Family reunification possible
  • No labor market test

General Employment Permit

  • For non-critical skills
  • Minimum salary €30,000
  • Labor market test required
  • Valid for 2 years
  • Renewable

Graduate Scheme

  • For recent graduates
  • Valid for 1-2 years
  • No minimum salary
  • Work in any sector
  • Path to work permit
